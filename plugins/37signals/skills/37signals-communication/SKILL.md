# 37signals Communication

Use this when the work product is communication: writing that preserves context,
reduces meetings, clarifies ownership, and lets people make or understand a
decision without another status loop.

## Workflow

1. Identify the audience and the decision or update they need.
2. Separate facts, tradeoffs, recommendation, and decision owner.
3. Prefer durable written context over synchronized status theater.
4. Make the ask explicit: decide, review, notice, or act.
5. Keep tone calm, concrete, and proportional to the stake.
6. Use `../../references/recipes/decision-memo.md` for decision artifacts.

## Defaults

- Lead with what changed or what decision is needed.
- Include enough context for a reader who missed the live discussion.
- Name owner, appetite, no-gos, and next checkpoint when relevant.
- Avoid performative urgency, vague consensus language, and process theater.

## Do Not Use For

- Cutting product scope: use `$37signals-scope-judgment`.
- Shaping a bet: use `$37signals-shape-up`.
- PR or code review: use `$37signals-rails-review`.

## Output

Return the communication artifact or rewrite plus the audience, decision owner,
and any missing fact that should be verified before sending.
