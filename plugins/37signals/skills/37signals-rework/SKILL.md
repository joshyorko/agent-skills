# 37signals Rework

Use this skill to make the work smaller, clearer, calmer, and more shippable. The job is not to make a plan sound like 37signals; it is to remove overkill and expose the few decisions that matter.

## Review Lens

- What can be removed without hurting the core customer outcome?
- Is this trying to look like a bigger company or product than it is?
- Does the plan create recurring coordination cost?
- Is a meeting, process, role, or dashboard compensating for unclear ownership?
- Can writing, a decision owner, or a smaller scope replace synchronization?
- Is the work deepening a valuable product area or widening the surface area?

## Defaults

- Prefer fewer features with sharper behavior.
- Prefer clear writing over status theater.
- Prefer decisions made by the person closest to the work.
- Prefer a fixed appetite and tradeoffs over elastic scope.
- Prefer asynchronous communication unless speed or ambiguity demands live discussion.
- Prefer a smaller product truth over a larger coordination story.
- For AI features, prefer one reviewable, source-backed output over dashboards, alerts, personalization settings, and autonomous actions.

## Output

Return the strongest simplification, what to cut or defer, the decision owner or next decision, the calmest execution path, and any risk created by cutting scope.

Avoid turning every disagreement into a philosophy essay. Be concrete about what changes.
