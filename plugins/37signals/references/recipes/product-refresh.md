# Product Refresh

Use this for existing screens, workflows, onboarding, settings, or features that
feel stale, bloated, confusing, or out of step with current product needs.

Do: narrow to one user-visible pain, preserve what works, find the epicenter,
set an appetite, cut stale surface area, write real copy, and design all states.

Avoid: vague modernization, visual-only redesign, rewrites, rebrands, new
frontend stacks, dashboards, settings, or roles before a bounded pain justifies
them.
